Analytical Testing

Fluid Energy is proud to offer a wide variety of Analytical Testing for our powder processing customers. We have developed over 100 ISO certified work instructions to meet our clients ever growing list of specifications.

Analytical Testing

We continually upgrade our equipment, and our equipment is calibrated quarterly using a third-party certified Lab Equipment calibration company.

Our lab is equipped, but not limited to the following testing devices:

  • Microtrac S3500 Particle size analysis (laser diffraction)
  • Moisture Measuring Equipment
  • Sieve analysis
  • Gas Chromatography
  • X-Ray Fluorescence
  • Drawdown gauges using Hegman, Micron and scale and NPIRI scales.
  • Air Jet Sieve Analysis
  • Optical Microscopes
  • Bulk Density
  • Moisture Balance
  • Color Spectrometers
  • pH measurement
  • Microbiological Testing

Fluid Energy management and lab personnel are continually trained in the latest techniques for analyzing our wide range of raw material and finished products.

We have successfully developed oil absorption testing, gravimetric testing solutions, ash testing, APD testing and many, many more unique analytical methods for our customers.

Fluid Energy will also provide our Analytical Services to companies that do not process with us in some cases. We are here for your analytical needs, so give us a call.
